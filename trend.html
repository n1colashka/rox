3D Elements The Comeback of 3D Elements

Okay… What is it Exactly?

There’s nothing revolutionary or innovative in using 3D elements in UI design. Actually these elements have been around for years and fascinated generations of users. 3D UI elements were trendy in the early days of computing, as they help users communicate with the offered functionality. However, today more and more websites and companies infuse those elements in their design, in the form of hyper-realistic 3D visuals.

This is an example of an informative website that with the help of 3D techniques become interactive and innovative.
So, What’s the Point?

This trend gives users a unique experience as they stretch the boundaries between real life and the digital realm and make them spend more time and increase engagement to meet business goals. However, for these to work, the UI should perform exceptionally well and be fast-loading, to properly deliver the desired effect. That being said, a lagging website due to content-loading problems might have the opposite effect and deter users from the website.

The 3D system gives the user the option to look at the bicycle from different angles.
The Takeaway

  • Mostly, the use of 3D elements in UI is for esthetic reasons.
  • 3D elements are just one way to make your product stand out.
  • Creating 3D elements with todays’ technologies is simple.

In this website, 3D creates a real-life experience of seeing a product in a storefront, as well as “trying it on” and experiencing how it looks on your body.
